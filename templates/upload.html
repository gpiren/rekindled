What is Rekindled?

Modern life is loud. Between the noise and the notifications, we forget the books that once moved us. The ideas that stayed with us for days, the sentences we wanted to remember forever.

But somewhere in your e-reader, those highlights are still there. The quotes that made you pause. The insights that changed how you think. Waiting to be found again.

Rekindled was built by people who care enough to highlight and who deserve more than a list of forgotten passages.

For non-fiction, we craft genre-aware, context-sensitive Memory Reports that distill the ideas you marked into something worth reading again.

For fiction, we let the words speak. Your highlighted passages, returned to you exactly as you found them, because some sentences need no explanation.
